    AUBSI Courses Registration Open
  • 19 Dec 2019
  • AMITY UNIVERSITY and BRITISH STANDARDS INSTITUTION (AUBSI) announce the commencement of the 26th batch of Professional Courses for the benefit of industry aspiring students. British Standards Institution is a Royal Charter Company of the UK and is a world leader in the field of system certifications and professional training. BSI India offers a whole range of world-class services for Management Systems Certifications, Trainings and Product Certifications. These courses which are globally recognized & internationally respected, have been reviewed as making students “INDUSTRY READY” thus enhancing their employability. Students may opt for any one of these courses as Open electives with 03 Credit Units in their APW on Amizone or as an Additional course. All the AUBSI classes will be conducted on 8 Saturdays for 4 hours. The following AUBSI (Amity University-BSI) courses are available for all students of Amity University: • Lean Six Sigma Green Belt • Quality Management Systems • Food Safety Management Systems • Information Security Management Systems • Facility Management Systems • Certified Automotive Quality Professional • Environmental Management Systems • Risk Management • Business Continuity Management • Energy Management Systems • Personal Information Mgmt. Systems • Social Accountability The details of these courses are available at www.aubsi.com . On qualification, the student acquires the certificate- ‘Certified Management System Professional’ which is jointly issued by BSI and Amity. The AUBSI courses are being offered at a highly subsidized fee and you will be required to pay a nominal fee of Rs. 6000/- (Six thousand only) per course. The student who selects an AUBSI course as an ‘Open Elective’, is required to make online payment of the AUBSI course fee, giving all his details on Amizone. After submitting Academic Programme Worksheet (APW), student needs to go to ‘My Courses’. Under AUBSI Open Elective, there is a link for payment, after which respective Head of Institution (HOI) gives the approval. Students opting for AUBSI Courses as ‘Additional’, need to fill up the Registration form, which is free of cost, (available with Ms. Shama Bhardwaj (Manager-Institutional Trainings, AUBSI Cell, Room No. 214, 2nd Floor, Block E 2, Amity University Campus, Sec-125, Noida) and make payment by Demand Draft in favour of ‘AMITY GLOBAL BUSINESS SCHOOL’ payable at Noida (to be deposited at the Accounts Department). It is mandatory for students to get their forms duly signed by their HOI/HOD before submission. The last date to register and submit the fee for 26th AUBSI Batch is Thursday, February 06, 2020. For any further information, query/ Brochure (free of cost), desirous students may contact / meet Ms. Shama Bhardwaj (Manager-Institutional Trainings, AUBSI Cell, Room No. 214, 2nd Floor, Block E 2, Amity University Campus, Sec-125, Noida or contact her by phone at 0120-4392447, 9810599007/ 7011258787 or by e-mail at aubsi@amity.edu. Shama Bhardwaj Manager-Institutional Trainings, AUBSI Cell

    NOTICE FOR ALL - PARKING OF VEHICLES ON THE ROADS OUTSIDE CAMPUS PREMISES
  • 18 Nov 2019
  • PARKING OF VEHICLES ON THE ROADS OUTSIDE CAMPUS PREMISES 1. It is reiterated with concern and also communicated by the Noida Authorities that large number of vehicles are haphazardly Parked / Standing outside the boundary of Amity University Uttar Pradesh, Sector-125, Noida Campus. This results in impeding free flow of traffic and security issues. Additionally, it also creates noise pollution and nuisance around the Campus. 2. This is for the information that the Traffic Police may tow-away the vehicles parked on the roads around the Campus boundary and also take legal action against the defaulters in accordance to the rules and regulations. 3. In view of the above, all Amitians are advised through this notification that no Faculty members, Staff & Students should park their Cars / Two Wheelers on the road around Amity University Uttar Pradesh, Sector-125, Noida Campus. 3. If anyone is doing so, they are doing it at their own risk. Amity University is neither responsible nor accountable for safety of such vehicles parked at unauthorized places. 4. Therefore, all Faculty members, Staff & Students are requested to park their vehicles at respective designated Parkings within the Campus premises including the Dell Complex Parking. 5. Further, in keeping with the Government Advisories in this regard, all are requested to make use of public transport / resort to pooling of vehicles for commuting to the Campus. 6. All Deans, Advisors and Heads of Institutions of Higher Education are requested to give wide publicity and sensitize respective Students and Faculty members that there is sufficient parking available within the Campus and not to park their vehicles on the Road outside the Campus boundary. 7. Further, Heads of the Institutions of Higher Education are also requested to provide feedback to this Office on action taken in this regard. 8. The University Management also requests the worthy Parents of our Students to sensitize their wards to utilize the Parking facilities available within the Campus premises for them and not to Park their vehicles on the Road outside Campus premises. (Ravi Chander Kochhar) Group Pro VC & OSD (HR)

    NOTICE FOR ALL - COMPULSORY WEARING OF AMITY ID CARDS AT THE CAMPUS
  • 01 Oct 2019
  • NOTICE FOR ALL - COMPULSORY WEARING OF AMITY ID CARDS AT THE CAMPUS 1. Wearing of Amity ID Cards is mandatory requirement for all Faculty members, Staff and Students while at the Campus premises at Amity University Campus. In that the ID Cards must be properly worn so as to prominently display for ease of identification & also for Security purposes. 2. However, it has been observed with concern that large number of Students are seen moving around the Academic Blocks & other common areas of the Campus without wearing their ID Cards. This serious lapse on the part of Students is against the basic norms of Student discipline and also has serious Security connotations. 3. In view of the above, Heads of all Institutions of Higher Education are requested to appropriately instruct respective Faculty Members, Programme Leaders, Mentors and Institution Level Proctors to sensitise the Students regarding the requirement of compulsory wearing of the Id Cards whilst at the Campus including in the Classrooms, Labs, Library, Cafeterias and other such places. 4. Further the HoIs and HoDs are also requested to ensure that the Programme Leaders and Course Faculty members regularly check the Students for wearing their Id Cards while taking the Classes and interacting with the Students during various occasions and also motivate them to take pride in wearing and displaying their Amity Id Cards which they have earned with years of hardwork. 5. Dy Dir Security is requested to instruct the Security Staff at the Gates to check and ensure that the Students wearing their Id Cards are only allowed to enter the Campus. They should be wearing their I-Cards while entering and leaving the Campus. 6. Proctor is requested to instruct members of the Proctorial Board and the Marshals to regularly check the Students and ensure that they properly wear their Id Cards within the Campus premises. 7. Random surprise check must be carried out by Special Team constituted by the HoIs / DSW. 8. All Advisors, Deans, HoIs, HoDs and Senior members RBEF are requested to also sensitise their respective Staff Members to properly wear their Amity Id Cards so as to prominently display them during their stay within the Campus premises. (Ravi Chander Kochhar) Group Pro VC & OSD (HR)
